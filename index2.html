The Rasta King

February 6, 1945 - Robert Nesta Marley is born in Nine Miles, St Ann's, Jamaica.
1962 - Records his first song, Judge Not, in Kingston with legendary record producer Leslie Kong.
1966 - He marries soul singer Rita Anderson and goes on to have five children, living in the US.
1971 - Marley forms his own record label, Tuff Gong.
1978 - He holds the One Love Peace Concert in Kingston.
May 11, 1981 - dies at age 36 in a US Hospital.
May 1986- The Marley museum is open in Kingston, on the site of his former home.
1990 - Marley's birthday is declared a national holiday in Jamaica.
1994 - Bob Marley is posthumously admitted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame.
